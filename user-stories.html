User Stories

General Story: I want to securely send encrypted text (private, sensitive, or random information) on the web.

In the Workplace

As a boss or employee of a big company, I want to send private information to certain employees or customers without worrying about it being hacked or seen by the wrong people.

As a political candidate, I want to send emails on my unsecure personal server without fear of its actual contents being leaked to the public.

As a legal or financial professional, I want to securely send highly personal information to clients.

I don't want to have to worry about accidentally sending sensitive information to the wrong person because the information has been encrypted.

Personal

I want to feel super cool and tech savvy by being able to send secure and encrypted text across the web.

I have my own sensitive information that I only want to send to certain people online.

I am paranoid about the government seeing my online messages or about my messages being stored on databases of big companies so I want to send encrypted information on the web that can't be seen by any third party (including the government).
